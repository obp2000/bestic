YoxView demo #2 - customizing with options

In this example, three options were used, to change the behavior and appearance of YoxView:

  • lang: "ja" - display labels and help in Japanese.
  • backgroundColor: "#000099" - YoxView's background is blue rather than the usual black.
  • autoPlay: true - YoxView opens in slideshow mode.
